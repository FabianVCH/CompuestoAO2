Recycling: Learn more about it

Recycling is a physicochemical process , mechanical or other work which involves subjecting a subject or a product already used ( junk ) , to a cycle of total or partial treatment to obtain a raw material or a new product. You could also be defined as the collection of raw materials from waste , introducing them back into the cycle of life and occurs at the prospect of depletion of natural resources , macro economic and effectively remove human waste not need.

Recycling Chain

recycling chain consists of several stages : Origin : it can be domestic or industrial . Recovery : it can be performed by public or private companies . Only consists in the collection and transportation of waste to the next link in the chain. Transfer plants : it is one link or volunteer who is not always used . Here are mixed waste for transport greater at lower cost (using larger containers or compactors more powerful ) . Plant classification (or separation ) which classifies the waste and separate the recoverable . Recycler end (or plant valuation ) where waste is eventually recycled ( paper , plastiqueros , etc. . ) , Stored (landfill ) or used for energy production ( cement , biogas, etc. . ) For domestic source separation used in different colored containers located in urban or rural environments : Yellow container ( containers ) : This is required to deposit all kinds of light packaging and plastic containers (bottles , jars , bags , trays, etc . ) Cans (drinks , preserves, etc. . ) Blue container ( paper and cardboard ) : In this container must be deposited cardboard containers ( boxes, trays, etc. . ) , As well as newspapers , magazines , wrapping papers , propaganda, etc. . It is advisable to fold boxes so that they occupy minimum space inside the container. Green container (glass) : This container is deposited glass containers. Gray container ( organic ): 1 In the deposited waste the rest do not belong in the above groups , mainly organic wastesclassified as biodegradable material mechanics . Container red ( hazardous waste) : As mobile phones, insecticides, batteries or batteries , cooking oil or cars , syringes , aerosol cans , etc. ...

Rule Three R

Recycling is part of the waste management strategy of the three Rs: Reduce, actions to reduce the production of objects that could become waste. Reuse, actions that allow the re-use a particular product to give it a second life, with the same or a different use. Recycle, all the operations of collection and treatment of waste which render reintroduce them into a cycle of life.
