¿Who we are?

We are 3 students who want to fight to seek new ways of how to help our planet. Either way, the three are united with what is art, whether through music or jugglery, therefore our mission is to look like to help our planet through art, and so achieve a better place to live

¿What is AO2?

The creation of AO2 is inspired by the art and all that it means, and we thought why not make art while caring for the environment?, Here arose the name of our page, that as our slogan says is a "composed of Art and Life"

AO2 Mision

Our mission is to teach people a new way of thinking, a new way of working and a new way of how to live we call "Be friendly, be creative!", This phrase which means for us is we can coexist friendly with the environment while you beautify it every day.
